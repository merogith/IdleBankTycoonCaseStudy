Selected projects

Idle Bank Tycoon — Game Economy Design Case Study Spreadsheet model · A/B design
  • Found the late-game problem in the numbers: by level 60, idle cash piles up to 4,900–21,600× the next upgrade's cost, so progression stops mattering. Traced it by rebuilding the full income-vs-cost curve in a parameter-driven model (400+ linked formulas, one inputs tab).
  • Designed two live-ops levers to fix it: a hard-currency liquidity lock and income-scaled rewarded-ad tuning. Modelled each to a blended ARPDAU +15.5% (~$2M/yr at static DAU), with a sensitivity grid and retention guardrails. My call: ship the ad tuning first and treat the loan as the bigger retention bet.
Free-to-Play Game Economy & Balance Python · scikit-learn · SQL
  • Caught two premium items quietly breaking the game (61% win rate, significant on a z-test), plus a slow coin inflation where sinks claw back only ~63% of what's minted. Each finding shipped with its balancing fix attached, not just a chart.
  • Flagged likely future payers from their first three days (AUC 0.80) using D1/D7/D30 cohorts, then turned that signal into a concrete onboarding lever.
US Stock-Market Valuation & Risk Python · statsmodels (OLS) · SQL
  • Showed across 150+ years that expensive markets pay you back less: higher valuation (CAPE) predicts lower forward 10-year real returns (p ≈ 1e-40), with the worst real drawdown a brutal −81%. I built it to drive an actual allocation call.
